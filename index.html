About me

Jangho Park is an assistant professor of Systems Management Engineering / Industrial Engineering at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU), where he teaches and conducts research on operations research, optimization, etc. Previously, he was an assistant professor at Hongik University in Seoul, Korea. Before moving back to South Korea, he was a data scientist at Walmart, San Francisco, CA, USA, and Teikametircs, Seattle, WA, USA, where he worked on advertising optimization, AdTech, and omnichannel advertising. Also, he was a postdoctoral researcher in the Computational Research Division at the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory. He received his Ph.D. in industrial engineering from The Ohio State University, Columbus, OH, USA, under supervision of Prof. Güzin Bayraksan, a master's degree in industrial engineering from Pohang University of Science and Technology, Pohang, South Korea, under supervision of Prof. Chi-Hyuck Jun, and a bachelor's degree in industrial engineering from Hanyang University, Seoul, South Korea.
